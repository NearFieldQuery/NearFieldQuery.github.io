What Is It?

Our unique solution pairs Near Field Communication (NFC) technology with blockchain-based smart contracts to deliver unprecedented business automation. Tokenize, Automate, and Query (TAQ) complex transactions in only a few effortless steps.

How It Works

  1. Initiate

    Tap your phone to a TAQ device to start a smart transaction.

  2. Identify

    The TAQ system automatically recognizes or creates your account using your digital ID*.

  3. Interface

    Access the smart contract library and business's assets on your device**.

  4. Input

    Choose the relevant smart contract and asset(s). Transactory details are queried from the token data or inputted by the user, if necessary.

  5. Initialize

    Once you confirm the agreement, it is sent to other nodes to process and validate.

In conclusion, our system packages complex business processes into a seamless, user-friendly experience. With just a tap and minimal inputs, the transaction is executed without paperwork and profiteering middlemen, saving you time and resources. Join us in revolutionizing business automation.

* A Digital ID (DiD) can be created in seconds for brand new users.

** A TAQ device can be preconfigured to one contract with multiple assets or to a specific asset with multiple agreement options, depending on the situation. This step can be automatic for purely access-based scenarios or to trigger functions.
